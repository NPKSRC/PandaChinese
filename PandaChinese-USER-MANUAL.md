# PandaChinese — User Guide

Learn Chinese vocabulary with **Bao**, your panda study buddy. Everything saves automatically on your device.

---

## Getting started

When you first open the app you create a **profile**: pick a name and a buddy animal. Each profile keeps its own progress (XP, streak, memory, game results), so family members can share one device with separate buddies. Tap the buddy/avatar in the top bar any time to switch profiles or add a new one.

At the top you'll also see the **course bar** — tap it to choose what you're studying: a single HSK level, a cumulative range (e.g. HSK 1–3), or the class-lessons course. Everything below adapts to the scope you pick.

The five tabs along the bottom are your main screens:

## 📖 Study — flashcards

This is the heart of the app. You see a card; you decide how well you knew the word, and the app schedules it for review accordingly (spaced repetition — words you find hard come back sooner).

- **Tap the card** to flip it and peek at the answer (pinyin, meanings, example).
- **Rate every word** with one of four buttons, which are always on screen:
  - **Again** — didn't know it (comes back in under a minute)
  - **Hard** — barely got it (review soon)
  - **Good** — got it
  - **Easy** — too simple
- **汉字 / 拼音 / Meaning first** chips choose which side shows first.
- **简 / 繁** chips switch between Simplified and Traditional characters.
- **🔀 Shuffle** reorders the deck; **↺ Restart** sends you back to the first card; **⏰ Study due** jumps to just the cards that are due for review.
- The speaker icon reads the word aloud. The arrows move between cards (you can also swipe).

As you rate words, your buddy grows — see Progress.

## ❓ Quiz — test yourself

Pick a quiz type (meaning, hanzi, or listening), and answer multiple-choice questions drawn from your current scope. At the end you get a score and a word of encouragement from your buddy. Quizzes are a good way to confirm what your flashcards have taught you.

## ☰ Browse — the word list

A scrollable list of every word in the current scope. Use the filter chips to show only **New / Learning / Familiar / Mastered** words — handy for seeing what you've still got to learn or for reviewing a specific group.

## 📈 Progress — your buddy's growth

- **Your buddy** grows through stages as you learn: Cub → Scout → Quick Learner → Scholar → Master → Sage.
- The **growth bar** shows how close you are to the next stage. When games have contributed, a second slim bar appears underneath splitting your growth into **Studying** (jade) and **Games** (amber).
- Stat tiles show **Total XP**, **Day streak**, **Words mastered**, and **Quiz accuracy**.
- The **memory map** breaks your current scope into New / Learning / Familiar / Mastered so you can see your coverage at a glance.

Tip: studying is what lifts your buddy to the highest stages — games help, but learning words is the real engine.

## 🎮 Games — play with your words

Practice the current scope through quick games:

- **Memory Match** — flip tiles and pair each word with its match.
- **Tone Hero** — see a character and its toneless pinyin, then tap the correct tone.

Clearing levels earns trophies and feeds a little extra growth to your buddy.

---

## Tips

- **Little and often beats cramming.** Do the **⏰ due** cards each day to keep your streak and let spaced repetition do the work.
- **Be honest with the rating buttons** — that's what makes review scheduling smart.
- **Switch first-side** (汉字 / 拼音 / Meaning) to test recall in both directions.
- Everything is saved on **this device** only. Clearing your browser data or using a different device/browser starts fresh.
